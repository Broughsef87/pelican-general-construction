Project Overview

Pelican General Construction served as the general contractor on a ground-up water treatment facility build, delivering both the structural concrete storage tank and the concrete masonry unit (CMU) pump house from foundation to finish.

The project required precise slip-form concrete work for the large-scale cylindrical tank — a specialized process that demands continuous pours and careful forming to achieve the structural integrity required for water containment infrastructure.
